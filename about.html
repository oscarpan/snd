About Us From our Crafties, to your crew.

Since 1997, Sunday Night Dinner Craft Services has been providing excellent Craft Services in Los Angeles Area and even out of California. We understand the needs of your Production.

In addition to taking care of your crew, we are experts at helping with additional client services such as Greenrooms, Cast and VIP needs, and all other aspects of production! Drop-Off Services is another excellent way to provide for your crew when you have tight budget. Our Crafties take pride in taking really good care of your crew.

Sunday Night Dinner Craft Services takes pride in maintaining a clean and orderly truck that matches the "Grade A" quality of a 5 star restaurant.

We are members of I.A.T.S.E. Local 80.

We provide Craft Services for both Union and Non-Union Shows.
